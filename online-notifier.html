Online Notifier is a simple, lightweight Discord bot that instantly notifies your server when someone joins a voice channel. It helps keep everyone informed and improves engagement in gaming, study, or chill servers by making it easier to see who's active.

Many Discord servers go quiet simply because members don't know who's online. That was a problem I faced in my own server â€” and that's where this bot idea comes to my mind. Online Notifier alerts everyone when someone joins a voice channel. This brings attention back to activity and encourages conversations and participation.

  • Sends a notification message when someone joins a voice channel.
  • If you don't want the bot to send messages for certain channels, simply hide those channels from the bot it won't see them.
  • Lightweight and fast.

After installing the bot to your server, set the notification channel using the command:
!setchannel in the desired channel, and the bot will start sending notifications there.
